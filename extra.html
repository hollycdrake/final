Creative Writing Club

In my sophmore year, my friends and I started the Creative Writing Club here at Mountain Vista!

In this club we:

  • Wrote (obviously)
  • Critiqued
  • Challenged ourselves with prompts
  • Entered competitions
  • Played writing games
  • Shared our work

As a result, I learned:

  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Poetry
  • How to present work
  • How to give and use helpful feedback
